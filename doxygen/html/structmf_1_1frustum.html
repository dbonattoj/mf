Detailed Description

Pyramid frustum, clipped by near and far planes.

Represented using the view projection matrix. Encodes both intrinsic (shape of frustum) and extrinsic (pose) parameters. Provides extraction of the 6 planes, the corners and edges, and intersection test with bounding box.
