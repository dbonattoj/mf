void mf::media_node::pull ( time_unit  target_time)
overridevirtual

Pull frames until target_time.

Pulls until target_time, or until end, whichever comes first. Implemented by subclass (media_sequential_node or media_parallel_node). Must ensure that after call, frame target_time will eventually be available in output buffer(s), and/or end time will be set in buffer(s). Cannot be called when reached_end() is true.
