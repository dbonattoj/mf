Detailed Description

template<std::size_t Dim>
class mf::ndarray_view_generic< Dim >

Generic ndarray_view where lower dimension(s) are type-erased.

Elements of ndarray_view_generic<Dim> represent entire k-dimensional sections (called frame) of an anterior ndarray_view<Dim + k, Elem>. This ndarray_view must have default strides (possibly with padding) for the first k dimensions. ndarray_view_generic preserves type information of T (std::type_info, along with its sizeof and alignof), its actual alignment (last stride of anterior ndarray_view), and number of elements per frame. Dimension and shape of frame is not preserved.

Generic ndarray view ndarray_view_generic<Dim> is internally represented as ndarray_view<Dim + 1, byte>. The last axis corresponds to the type-erased k-dimensional frame, represented as raw byte array. Its shape and stride are the number and alignment of T elements in the frame.

Subscripting results in another ndarray_view_generic of lower dimension.
